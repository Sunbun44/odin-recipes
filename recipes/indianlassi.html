Indian Lassi

Description

/home/plumbalenda82/repos/odin-recipes/images/japanese_curry.png

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups plain yogurt
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed
  • 1 ½ cups ice water
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. In a blender, blend the yogurt, ice, water, sugar and salt until mixture becomes frothy. Pour mixture over ice cubes in tall glasses.
